# Insights & Business Findings — Global Skincare E-Commerce

## 1. Overall KPIs
- **Total Sales:** $6,517,674  
- **Total Profit:** $1,065,414  
- **Average Discount:** 14.3%  
- **Orders:** 25,728  
- **Customers:** 17,415  
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** $253.33  
- **Repeat Customer Rate:** 31.2%

**Interpretation:**  
The business shows strong overall performance with a healthy customer base and solid retention. An AOV above $250 indicates high-value purchases, typical for skincare and beauty e-commerce.

---

## 2. Category Performance

| Category | Sales | Profit | Profit Margin |
|----------|--------:|---------:|--------------:|
| Body care | 2,682,942 | 590,908 | 22.0% |
| Home & Accessories | 1,245,643 | -57,188 | **-4.6%** |
| Make up | 1,121,222 | 368,998 | 32.9% |
| Hair care | 1,022,151 | 3,677 | 0.36% |
| Face care | 445,716 | 159,018 | **35.7%** |

**Insights:**  
- **Body Care** drives the highest revenue but not the highest profit.  
- **Face Care** and **Make Up** deliver the strongest profitability.  
- **Home & Accessories is unprofitable**, likely due to high discounts or low margins.  
- Hair Care revenue is strong but margin is extremely low, indicating a pricing issue.

## 4. Anti-Aging Analysis

- **Anti-aging sales:** $140,206  
- **Share of total sales:** 2.15%

**Insights:**  
Anti-aging represents a small share of total revenue but is known to have high consumer demand and high-margin potential. This category has strong growth potential with the right marketing and bundling strategies.

---

## 5. Discount Impact on Profitability

Profit margin decreases significantly as discount levels increase:

| Discount Bucket | Profit Margin |
|------------------|----------------:|
| **0%** | **31.7%** |
| 0–10% | 19.0% |
| 10–20% | 11.9% |
| 20–30% | 5.3% |
| **30%+** | **-22.0%** |

**Insights:**  
Deep discounts (especially above 30%) are highly unprofitable.  
A better strategy is to maintain discounts **below 20%** and focus on value-added promotions rather than aggressive price cuts.

---

## 6. Customer Insights

- Strong repeat purchase rate (**31.2%**)  
- High-value customers contribute significantly to revenue  
- Many customers purchase from multiple categories  
- Opportunity to build loyalty programs, subscriptions, or VIP tiers

---

## 7. Market & Region Performance

Some markets perform significantly better both in revenue and profitability.  
A targeted pricing or marketing strategy per region could substantially improve margins.

---

## 8. Final Recommendations

1. Reduce focus or optimize **Home & Accessories**, which is currently losing money.  
2. Double down on **Make Up** and **Face Care**, which deliver the highest margins.  
3. Invest in **Anti-Aging** category through bundles, retinol/collagen-based lines, and influencer campaigns.  
4. Redesign discount strategy — avoid discounts above **20%**, especially 30%+.  
5. Introduce **Loyalty/VIP programs** to increase retention and Lifetime Value (LTV).  
6. Improve regional segmentation with targeted promotions and pricing.
